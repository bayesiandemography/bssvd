# Census Data on School Attendance

Data on school attendance rates, by age and sex, from censuses,
assembled by the United Nations Statistics Division.

## Usage

``` r
un_csa_subset
```

## Format

A tibble with 7668 rows and with columns "Country or Area", "Year",
"Area", "Sex", "Age", "School attendance", "Record Type", "Reliability",
"Source Year", "Value", and "Value Footnotes"

## Source

Downloaded from table "Population 5 to 24 years of age by school
attendance, sex and urban/rural residence" from the "Population
Censuses' Datasets (1995 - Present)" in the [Demographic Statistics
Database](https://unstats.un.org/unsd/demographic-social/products/dyb/index.cshtml).
Accessed 22 October 2025.
